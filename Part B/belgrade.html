City of Belgrade


Belgrade is the capital city of the Republic of Serbia and it is divided into 17 municipalities which 10 of them are urban and 7 of them are suburban municipalities. Belgrade is one of the most populous cities in Serbia and it is the largest one.


The city has deep historical roots and is one of the oldest settlements in Europe, like Ä°stanbul and Athens. Some relics found in the surrounding area date back to periods between 20,000 to 50,000 years ago, proving the ancient history of the city.


With its lively nightlife, tourist attractions and historic beauties, the city of Belgrade should be in your travel bucket-list. Whether you like nature or architecture, adventure or a relaxed vacation; Belgrade has something to offer.
